Comparison Narrative

This comparison covers Update 6 (Sep 1, 2025) to Update 7 (Jan 1, 2026) for the Downtown Medical Center Tower project.

Schedule Performance

The critical path has slipped by 18 working days between updates, driven primarily by delays in the curtain wall installation and MEP rough-in activities. The project's forecast substantial completion date has moved from October 21, 2026 to November 14, 2026, a 24-working-day slip against the prior update.

Validation Score

The overall validation score declined by 1 point from 72 to 71, remaining in the Amber range. The primary drivers of score deterioration are (1) an increase in negative float activities from 18 to 23, indicating growing schedule pressure, and (2) continued open-end logic issues in the MEP and structural scopes.

Key Changes

87 activities experienced date changes during this period. Notable changes include the chiller plant installation, which shifted +45 days, and curtain wall activities on levels 12-16, which shifted +40 days to finish. 12 new activities were added and 4 were deleted, reflecting scope clarifications in the MEP package.

Recommended Actions

The project team should prioritize: (1) investigating the cause of the 18-day critical path slip and developing a recovery schedule; (2) addressing the 5 newly negative float activities with constraint or logic remediation; and (3) closing 8 new open-end logic gaps introduced since the last update.
