Features

Everything a P6 scheduler needs

Built on the DCMA 14-point assessment — extended with six weighted scoring categories for deeper insights.

📊

DCMA+ Quality Score

Instantly score your schedule across six weighted categories — logic, constraints, critical path, activity hygiene, date integrity, and progress realism — on a 0–100 scale.

🔴

Critical Path Analysis

Identify near-critical activities, negative float, and critical path slippage before they become project delays. Visual heat-maps highlight the highest-risk activities.

🔗

Logic & Constraint Diagnostics

Detect open ends, missing predecessors, lags, leads, and hard constraints. Every finding links back to the specific DCMA check it fails.

📈

Version Comparison

Upload two schedule updates and see exactly what changed — score deltas, slipping milestones, new open ends — side by side in a clean comparison view.

📝

Narrative Reports

Generate a plain-English schedule narrative that summarises key findings and recommendations — ready to paste into your monthly report or owner update.

🎓

Built-in Learning Centre

Every metric and diagnostic links to a searchable knowledge base explaining what it means, why it matters, and how to fix it — great for junior schedulers.
