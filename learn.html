Scoring Methodology

DCMA+ Schedule Quality Scoring

The Defense Contract Management Agency (DCMA) developed a 14-point schedule quality assessment in 2005. Construct Check extends this into six weighted categories producing a 0–100 quality score. Every diagnostic finding maps back to one or more of the original 14 DCMA checks.

25% Logic Quality
20% Constraints & Float
15% Critical Path
15% Activity Hygiene
15% Date Integrity
10% Progress Realism
🟢 Green ≥ 85
🟡 Amber 70–84
🔴 Red < 70
